# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **Google Analytics digital marketing data** to identify campaign performance, user behavior, conversion trends, and revenue opportunities.

The project combines **SQL, Power BI, DAX, and Business Analytics** to transform raw marketing data into interactive dashboards and strategic business recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Marketing teams generate huge volumes of campaign data, but converting that data into actionable insights is often challenging.

This project helps answer critical business questions:

- Which marketing channels generate the highest ROI?
- Which landing pages convert the best?
- Which audience segments generate the most revenue?
- Where are users dropping off in the conversion funnel?
- How can marketing budgets be optimized?

# 📑 Dashboard Pages

## 📊 Executive Overview

KPIs

- Total Users
- Total Sessions
- Total Revenue
- Revenue per Session
- Cost per Mile

Insights

- Organic Search drives the highest traffic.
- Indonesia, USA, and Qatar contribute the most revenue.
- Checkout page experiences significant user drop-off.

---

## 📈 Acquisition & Channel Insights

KPIs

- New Users
- Marketing Spend
- Impressions
- Engagement Rate
- Average Engagement Time

Insights

- Direct and Organic Search dominate acquisition.
- Some channels have high impressions but low conversions.
- High-engagement channels represent growth opportunities.

---

## 🌐 Landing Page Performance

KPIs

- Sessions
- Revenue
- Conversion Rate
- Revenue per Session
- Key Events

Insights

- Revenue follows the Pareto principle (80/20).
- Significant funnel leakage occurs after product views.
- Direct traffic contributes the majority of sessions.

---

## 👥 Audience & Demographics

KPIs

- Total Users
- Top Country
- Highest Revenue Age Group
- Engagement Rate

Insights

- Users aged 18–24 generate the highest revenue.
- Technology and Finance are the most common interests.
- Gender data requires improvement due to many unknown values.

---

## 🚀 Events Funnel & Forecasting

KPIs

- Total Events
- Predictive Users
- Funnel Drop-off Rate
- Engagement Events

Insights

- Large drop-off between View Item and Add to Cart.
- Purchasers contribute the highest revenue.
- Predictive analytics indicates strong growth potential.

# 📈 Business Insights

✅ Organic Search is the highest-performing acquisition channel.

✅ Revenue is concentrated in Indonesia, USA, and Qatar.

✅ Checkout pages have the highest abandonment rate.

✅ Direct traffic dominates overall sessions.

✅ Younger audiences (18–24) generate the highest revenue.

✅ Technology and Finance users show the strongest engagement.

✅ Funnel analysis reveals major conversion opportunities.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing channels.
- Optimize checkout experience to reduce abandonment.
- Improve underperforming landing pages.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-value audience segments.
- Use predictive insights for smarter budget allocation.
